Admissions

Admissions at Mehran University of Engineering & Technology (MUET) Jamshoro are open to talented students from across Pakistan. The university offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering and technology fields, with a transparent and merit-based selection process.

How to Apply

Applications open in June each year. Follow the online application process via the MUET Admission Portal.
